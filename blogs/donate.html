SBST SOCIAL OUTREACH & SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Irony of Skilled Labor Shortage in India

Despite millions registered in employment exchanges, very few get jobs due to outdated skills and lack of practical training. With 75% of future jobs being skill-based, SBST bridges this gap by offering relevant vocational training using available infrastructure.

For Women

SBST empowers women through SHGs, microcredit, vocational training, and entrepreneurship programs. These initiatives help women gain financial independence and build sustainable livelihoods while addressing health, sanitation, and education needs.

For Youth

Youth programs are designed with a focus on job-market needs, starting from identifying available jobs to providing placement-ready training. SBST ensures that trainees receive recognized qualifications to secure meaningful employment.

SBST Bridging Skills Gap

SBST connects industry demands with skilled manpower by forming partnerships with industries, NGOs, and government bodies. This ensures sustainable skill development aligned with national growth goals.
