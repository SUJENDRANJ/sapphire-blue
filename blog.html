INSIDE SBST INITIATIVES

Latest Activities & Field Experiences

2025

  • WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
    By: SBST Team, Jan 05, 2025

    Entrepreneurship Development Training for SC Women

    SBST organized a training program in Pennagaram, Dharmapuri district to empower SC women with Handloom weaving, and Textile skills for Self-Employment and their Placements.

  • HEALTH & MEDICAL CAMPS
    By: SBST Team, Jan 23, 2025

    Eye Camps for rural Villagers

    In collaboration with Erode Eye Foundation, SBST provided free eye screening, diagnosis, and treatment to hundreds of rural villagers in Erode district.

  • EDUCATION & TRAINING
    By: SBST Team, Feb 12, 2025

    TN-skill TNSDC Kalaignar Kaivinai Thittam (KKT) Training Across Districts in Tamil Nadu

    SBST trained start-up and small Entrepreneurs across Erode, Salem, Krishnagiri, etc... to upskill them with advanced technologies to support their businesses.

2024

  • WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
    By: SBST Team, July 2024

    Workshop on Imitation Jewellery Making

    Conducted in Erode & Salem districts, the workshop trained rural women in jewellery design, enabling them to earn through handmade products.

  • HEALTH & MEDICAL CAMPS
    By: SBST Team, April 2024

    Medical Camp in association with Nandha Siddha Medical College and Hospital at erode district

    Free health check-ups and medicines were provided to underprivileged rural families, improving overall community health outcomes.

  • EDUCATION & TRAINING
    By: SBST Team, Oct 2024

    Sericulture Awareness Program

    SBST has conducted Sericulture Awareness Program at Venkateshwara College, Gobi, students became aware of the business opportunities in sericulture including Silk Production, Marketing and Entrepreneurship.

2023

  • Education & Training
    By: SBST Team, 2023

    Awareness Program on Sericulture and seri-business opportunities

    SBST has conducted a two-day awareness program on Sericulture and seri-business opportunities at Erode. To enable the Farmers and Students to learn about the business opportunities in Sericulture.

  • Health and Hygiene
    By: SBST Team, 2023

    Health and Hygiene Awareness Program

    SBST organized awareness programs across Erode to educate communities on health, hygiene, environmental conservation, water scarcity, and sustainable water management practices.

  • Skill Development
    By: SBST Team, 2023

    Hand Block Printing Training

    SBST conducted a free hands-on Hand Block Printing training for artisians of Erode district. The event was held in Sapphire Blue Social Trust Training Centre, Erode, Tamil Nadu.

2022

  • Skill Development
    By: SBST Team, 2022

    Pattern Making & Tailoring Training

    SBST organized pattern making and tailoring training in Erode to empower women, especially from SC and backward communities.

  • Rural Development
    By: SBST Team, 2022

    Distribution of Utensils

  • Education
    By: SBST Team, 2022

2020 - 2021

    By: SBST Team
