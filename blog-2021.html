  • WOMEN EMPOWERMENT
  • Tamil Nadu - 2021

3,000+ Women & Youths Placed or Self-Employed

SBST empowered over 3,000 women, youth, and SC individuals through employment and self-employment initiatives across Erode, Salem, Tiruppur, Coimbatore, Namakkal, Karur, and the Nilgiris.

  • Health & Medical Camps
  • Erode - 2021

Free Ambulance & PPE Kits During COVID

SBST arranged free ambulance services and distributed protective gear to frontline workers and ambulance drivers during the pandemic to ensure public safety and medical access.

  • Education & Training
  • Erode - 2021

Skill Development for 4000 Artisans

SBST partnered with the Ministry of Textiles to train 4000 individuals under the SAMARTH scheme, aiming to revive the endangered silk handloom weaving industry.

  • SC/ST Community Support
  • Erode & Other Districts - 2021

Skill Development for SC & Backward Communities

SBST conducted specialized entrepreneurship and skill development training programs to uplift women, SC, and backward community members across Tamil Nadu.

  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Erode District - 2021

5,000 Trees Planted for Greener Tomorrow

SBST led a large-scale tree plantation campaign involving young volunteers. Over 5000 saplings were planted to combat air pollution and promote climate resilience.

Sapphire Blue Social Trust is committed to empowering marginalized communities through education, health, and skill development programs across Tamil Nadu.
